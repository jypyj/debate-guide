STEP 6

Expressions — 표현 참고

토론에서 활용할 수 있는 핵심 표현들입니다. 이 표현들을 사용하면 의사소통 전략 점수에 도움이 됩니다!

1. 내 의견 전달하기

  • I think that...
  • In my opinion, ...
  • I believe that...
  • From my perspective, ...
  • As far as I'm concerned, ...

2. 동의할 때

  • I agree with your point.
  • That's a good point.
  • I completely agree with you.
  • I see what you mean.
  • You're absolutely right.

3. 공손하게 반대하기

  • I understand your point, but I disagree.
  • I see your perspective, but I think differently.
  • I respectfully disagree.
  • I understand, but...
  • I see your point, however...

4. 논리적으로 반박하기

  • However, I think there is a problem with that idea.
  • I don't think that's always true.
  • That may be true, but…
  • On the other hand, ...
  • I'd like to point out that...

5. 질문·확인하기

  • What do you mean by that?
  • Can you explain more?
  • Could you give an example?
  • Why do you think so?

6. 연결·마무리

  • In addition, / Also, / Furthermore,
  • For example, / Because of this,
  • To sum up, ...
  • In conclusion, ...
  • Overall, I believe...

★ 핵심 전략

① 의견 + 이유 구조"I think ... because ..." 형식으로 말하기
② 연결어 + 근거 사용First, ... / Second, ... / Finally, ...
③ 상대방 존중 필수!"You are wrong" ✗ → "I see your point, but..." ✓
④ 끝까지 듣고 반응하기상대 말 끊지 않기 — 토론의 기본 예절
