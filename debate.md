## STEP 1. Choose the Topic

AI와 관련된 토론 주제를 선택하세요. 스스로 떠올린 아이디어가 가장 좋지만, 아래 리스트를 참고해도 됩니다. 생성형 AI의 도움을 받아도 됩니다!

### 🎓 EDUCATION
- Can AI replace teachers?
- Is it ethical to use AI to monitor student behavior and performance?
- Should AI-generated work be considered plagiarism?
- Should AI tools be allowed in school assessments and exams?
- Will dependency on AI tools make students less creative or critical?

### 💼 WORK
- Is it ethical for companies to replace human workers with AI?
- Is it okay to use AI in the hiring process?
- Should companies use more AI programs to replace employees and cut the budget?

### 🎨 ART
- Is AI art really art?
- Should AI-generated content be protected by copyright?
- Is AI creativity fundamentally different from human creativity?
- Who gets the rights to content created by AI? AI, the developer, or the person who put the prompt?

### 🌐 SOCIETY
- What are the professions that can or cannot be replaced by AI?
- Does AI really make people more productive?
- Is AI an environmentally sustainable tool?
- Should governments limit the use of AI to protect human jobs?
- Does AI increase social inequality between rich and poor?

> 주제가 정해지면 친구들과 함께 그 주제에 관해 이야기를 나눠봅시다.

## STEP 6. Expressions

토론에서 활용할 수 있는 핵심 표현들을 익혀봅시다.

### 1. 내 의견을 전달할 때
> 내 생각을 명확하게 표현하세요.

- I **think** that AI should not replace human workers.
- **In my opinion**, AI can be helpful in many ways.
- I **believe** that this is an important issue.
- **From my perspective**, AI is not always reliable.
- **As far as I'm concerned**, companies should be careful.

---

### 2. 상대방 의견에 동의할 때
> 상대방을 존중하면서 공감을 표현하세요.

- I **agree** with your point.
- That's a **good point**.
- I **completely agree** with you.
- I **see what you mean**.
- You're **absolutely right**.
- That **makes sense**.

> **e.g.** *I agree with you because .....*

---

### 3. 상대방 의견에 동의하지 않을 때 (공손하게!)
> 의견에 동의하지 않더라도 예의 있게 반대하세요.

- I understand your point, but **I disagree**.
- I see your perspective, but **I think differently**.
- That's interesting, but **I don't agree**.
- I'm not sure **I agree with that**.
- **I respectfully disagree.**

> **핵심패턴! 공감하며 반대하기**
> - **I understand, but...**
> - **I see your point, however...**

---

### 4. 의견에 반박할 때 (논리적으로)
> 근거를 들어 반박하기

- **However**, I think there is a problem with that idea.
- I **don't think that's always true**.
- **That may be true, but…**
- **On the other hand**, AI can cause problems.
- I'd like to point out that AI can make mistakes.

---

### 5. 질문하거나 확인할 때
> 토론을 이어가는 핵심 기술!

- **What do you mean** by that?
- **Can you explain more?**
- **Could you give an example?**
- **Why do you think so?**

---

### 6. 대화 이어가기 / 연결하기
> 자연스럽게 말 이어가기

`In addition,` / `Also,` / `Furthermore,` / `For example,` / `Because of this,` / `As a result,`

---

### 7. 마무리 / 정리하기

- **To sum up**, AI has both advantages and disadvantages.
- **In conclusion**, we should use AI carefully.
- **Overall**, I believe AI can be beneficial.
